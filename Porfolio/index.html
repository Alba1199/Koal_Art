·ABOUT ME·

Name: Alba García Rodriguez
Birthday: 24 December 1996
Nationality: Spain

I'm a Visual Design Student, born in Spain. I like to do a little bit of everything, from 3D Modeling to 2D Illustration. I'll like to grow a bit in this industry so I can inspire people as people inspire me.

I worked in Grupo SM as a design intern in the area of interactive technology innovation.

Also, developed a videogame called Project Katharsis with a group of friends, as an art coordinator and 3D Modeler. The first chapter of the game is currently out on Steam as a free to play.
